Our History
Like any other unique place, we have our own special story. The idea for our restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. During a walk in the forest, the creator of our restaurant found themselves stranded hundreds of kilometers away from the nearest town. Far from civilization and communication, they had to improvise and set up a modest life for themselves, procuring and cooking their own food
